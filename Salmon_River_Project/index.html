Fun for All Abilities

Everybody with a sense of adventure can go river rafting in Idaho. Using our knowledge of the rapids, we tailor our trips for all skill levels. Beginners will love our white water river rafting adventures on the calmer parts of the river which also have a little bit of excitment built in. Those that are able to experience a higher thrill level can select a section of the river that can be more difficult. Enjoy the beautiful scenic backdrop of the mountains and foothills as you bond with your fellow rafters and discover what makes white water rafting in Idaho so special.
